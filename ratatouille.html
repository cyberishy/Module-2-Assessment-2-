Ratatouille

Remy, a rat with a refined palate, dreams of becoming a chef. When he finds himself in Paris, he befriends Linguini, a young chef, and helps him create culinary masterpieces. Together, they defy the odds and show that anyone can cook.

Main Actors: Patton Oswalt, Ian Holm
