About Pine City Mall

Pine City Mall has been the heart of our community for over two decades. Offering a blend of shopping, dining, and entertainment, the mall is the perfect place for families, friends, and visitors to gather. With a variety of stores ranging from fashion to electronics, and dining options to suit every palate, Pine City Mall is your one-stop destination.

We believe in providing a top-notch experience for all our visitors, which is why we continuously strive to update our services and offerings to meet the needs of our diverse clientele. We are proud to be a part of this community and look forward to welcoming you.

Our Mission

To provide a welcoming, inclusive, and dynamic shopping experience for all our visitors, while supporting the local community and fostering a culture of sustainability.

Our Vision

To be the premier shopping destination in the region, recognized for our exceptional service, diverse offerings, and commitment to the community.
